ABOUT

BUMBA is a multipurpose web application which makes your time on the web productive. It eases a variety
of your tasks and saves a lot of your time. Have a look at its features

BookMarks
Todo List
GMail
Doodle

Manage All Your Bookmarks Under One Place

Sort all your bookmarks under several categories, storing them in an efficient way.
Add new categories & Drag and Drop bookmarks related to those categories. Its as simple as that.

Keep Track Of All Important Tasks

Add any task you need to do
Change priorities of certain tasks, move them up or bring them down
Mark tasks as done and delete them if wanted. The user friendly UI is self-explanatory though.

Get notified of any new Emails

Look out for new emails category wise here it self instead of visiting the website.
A must have feature for any desktop application

Doodle Out Imp Figures,Tables

Draw them out using your stylus/mouse/Touch pad
and save them for any future reference
